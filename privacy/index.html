Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Presidential Countdown ("the App") is provided as an ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled.

Information Collection and Use

The developer does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All countdown functionality operates locally on the user’s device.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and use certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers (for example, Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Crash and performance data

This information is used to:

  • Serve and personalize ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Improve ad quality

The developer does not have access to, store, or control this data.

Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob

Log Data

The developer does not collect log data. However, Google AdMob may collect limited technical information for diagnostics and ad performance.

Cookies

The App itself does not use cookies. Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies.
